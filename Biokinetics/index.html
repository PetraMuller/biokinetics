Life through movement


When we hear "Biokinetics" we think of rugby players, cricket bowlers or other athletes who need rehabilitation due to injuries. We very seldome concider the prevention of every day injuries such as back pain or neck pain.

At Albrie Labuschagne Biokinetics Practice we focus not only on rehabilitation or prevention of sport injuries, but also on the benefit of correct posture in the classroom, work place, and on the sport field. You will be amazed at how correct posture can increase performance and prevent injuries.

Advantages of correct posture includes:

  1. It Facilitates Breathing
  2. Increase concentration and thinking
  3. Improves self-image
  4. Prevents health complication, back pain, muscle strain and discomfort.
